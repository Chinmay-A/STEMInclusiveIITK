About Us

STEMInclusive IITK Chapter is a platform dedicated to celebrating the incredible achievements and stories of women of IIT Kanpur in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics). We believe in the power of representation, storytelling, and community to inspire the next generation of women in India to pursue their dreams in STEM fields.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to shatter stereotypes, break down barriers, and pave the way for women to thrive in STEM. We are committed to creating a world where every young girl in India knows that her potential is limitless, regardless of her gender.

Why STEMInclusive?

In India, as in many parts of the world, women have been historically underrepresented in STEM. We believe that this gender gap not only deprives our society of diverse perspectives and innovation but also limits the potential of countless talented individuals. STEMInclusive aims to change this by:

  1. Spotlighting Remarkable Women: We showcase the stories of extraordinary women who have excelled in various STEM fields. Through interviews, articles, and profiles, we bring their journeys, challenges, and achievements to the forefront.
  2. Providing Role Models: We firmly believe that you cannot be what you cannot see. By featuring accomplished women in STEM, we provide role models for young girls to look up to, demonstrating that they too can excel in these fields.
  3. Building a Supportive Community: STEMInclusive is not just a website; it's a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for equality and inclusion in STEM. We encourage networking, mentorship, and collaboration among women in STEM and those aspiring to join the field.
  4. Encouraging STEM Education: We actively conduct STEM education initiatives and workshops to ensure that every girl with an interest in STEM has the resources and opportunities to succeed.
